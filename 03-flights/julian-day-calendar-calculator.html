Julian Day Calendar Calculator

Main

This is a simple Julian Day Calendar Calculator that converts normal calendar dates into Julian days
that are used in BCBP Boarding Passes. The leap year days are in square brackets.

January 31 ----- 31
February 28 ----- 59
February 29 ----- [60]
March 31 ----- 90 [91]
April 30 ----- 120 [121]
May 31 ----- 151 [152]
June 30 ----- 181 [182]
July 31 ----- 212 [213]
August 31 ----- 243 [244]
September 30 ----- 273 [274]
October 31 ----- 304 [305]
November 30 ----- 334 [335]
December 31 ----- 365 [366]

An example: To get the julian day for June 15th, look for the last day in the previous month, which is
May 31st, then add 15. That is 151+15=166. [In leap years this will be 152+15=167.]
