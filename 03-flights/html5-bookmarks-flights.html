Flight Departure Advice

The information on Arrivals and Departures may not always be 100% accurate on third party websites,
and other information such as Terminals, Gates, and flight delays may not be shown at all. If you have
booked a flight, it is recommended to check if everything is OK by contacting the Airport or Airline
directly, either by their website to look at the Live Departures Boards from there, or contact them
directly by phone. Related website links include:
